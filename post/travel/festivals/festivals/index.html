Festivals : No Mames trip

by Parul

1 mins

I wanted to name this trip something. We have been on the road for over a year now and the trip needed a referral name. Also because Karan had already taken a break before, the references were getting confusing. We had shortlisted two options:

  • No Mames which means stop sucking in Spanish
  • Lekker which means almost anything nice in Dutch.

After a lot of consideration and discussions the trip from now on will be called as No mames trip.
